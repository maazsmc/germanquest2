01. Overview & App Identity

Welcome to German Quest ("we," "our," or "the Platform"). German Quest is a gamified language learning system designed to help users conquer German vocabulary through interactive quests, boss battles, levels, XP rewards, and AI tutor support.

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any Personal Data you share with us. This Privacy Policy outlines how your information is accessed, securely managed, and preserved when you link your Google Account to synchronize your vocabulary progress with Google Sheets.

02. How We Access & Use Google User Data

German Quest uses Google OAuth (Google Identity Services) strictly to power optional background cloud sync features. We request only the absolute minimum permissions required to perform integration functions. Below is a transparent breakdown of the specific Google Scopes requested and why they are vital to your experience:

profile

Basic Profile Access

Retrieves your Google username and profile picture. This is used strictly to dynamically construct your custom in-game RPG Character Card (displaying your character name and avatar profile ranks).

email

Email Address Verification

Retrieves your valid primary email address. This is utilized to safely link and securely back up your learning statistics (level, XP, streaks, weak words, coins) onto our cloud database.

spreadsheets & drive.file

Google Sheets & Drive Integrations

Used solely to read and write your learning records directly with a spreadsheet stored securely in your personal Google Drive account. This allow us to save your words, translations, and mastery levels directly onto your dynamic "German Quest Vocabulary Ledger" spreadsheet so you have absolute offline ownership of all study sheets. We do not modify or touch other files on your Drive folder.

03. Strict Data Privacy Policies

  • ⚔️
    No Sharing with Third Parties We do not sell, rent, license, trade, or share any Google User Data (including email addresses, profile pictures, or Sheets contents) with third-party advertisers, analytical brokers, or commercial marketing companies.
  • ⚔️
    Isolated Sandbox Execution Google tokens remain entirely isolated inside your private web browser's Secure LocalStorage and standard temporary runtime memories; they are used only to call authorized endpoints over SSL/HTTPS.
  • ⚔️
    Human-in-the-Loop Privacy Your study database, vocabularies, and user metrics are accessible purely to you, ensuring your educational path remains fully private and safe.

04. Data Security & Storage Actions

Your dynamic vocabulary progress is preserved locally via browser cache storage engines, as well as backed up onto a secure node database instance protected behind Cloud Run routing mechanisms.

All direct integrations and OAuth API exchanges are requested solely utilizing secure, modern Transport Layer Security protocol (HTTPS/TLS v1.3 with advanced certificate encryptions). This prevents eavesdropping, tampering, or credential interception by malicious actors.

05. Your Rights & Access Revocation

You have absolute control over your personal data. You are entitled to disconnect Google OAuth integrations or request the system to completely erase your database record at any time.
